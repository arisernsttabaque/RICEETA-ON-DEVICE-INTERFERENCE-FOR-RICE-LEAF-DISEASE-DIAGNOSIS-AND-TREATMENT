Brown Spot

Brown spot has been historically largely ignored as one of the most common and most damaging rice diseases. Brown spot has been historically largely ignored as one of the most common and most damaging rice diseases. What it does. Brown spot is a fungal disease that infects the coleoptile, leaves, leaf sheath, panicle branches, glumes, and spikelets.. Its most observable damage is the numerous big spots on the leaves which can kill the whole leaf.
